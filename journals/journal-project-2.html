Slash or Dash — PVP Reflex Game

About the Game

Slash or Dash is a fast-paced, offline 1v1 samurai-themed reflex game with a top-down 2D perspective. Players control their samurai by swiping — aiming to strike through their character and hit the opponent. But there's a twist: a well-timed parry can stun the attacker, creating a moment of vulnerability and shifting the tide of the duel. With quick, best-of-five rounds, this game is designed for local head-to-head fun on a shared screen, offering intense, smart, and often hilarious showdowns.

Release Plans

Slash or Dash is currently unreleased, intended as a small, fun party game and conversation starter. While there's interest in adding more content — such as an online ranking system and expanded features — the game remains a compact offline experience for now.

Future updates may depend on interest, but for now, it's a satisfying, minimalist take on reaction-based dueling.
