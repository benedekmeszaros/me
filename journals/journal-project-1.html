Contrast — Open World Roguelike Platformer

About the Game

Contrast is a 2D open-world roguelike platformer set in a mysterious void, where survival depends on your skill, strategy, and reflexes. In this unforgiving monochrome world, you must place color-coded platforms to stay alive and fight off relentless waves of enemies. The unique twist? The color of the platform you're standing on directly influences who you can damageâ€”and who can damage you. Precision, planning, and fast thinking are key if you want to reach the highest level and escape the endless void. Combining retro 8-bit aesthetics with modern gameplay mechanics,

Contrast offers a high-stakes, replayable experience where every run is different. With procedurally generated worlds, upgradeable characters and abilities, and punishing permadeath, each attempt to break free feels intense and fresh.

Key Features

  • Unique Color-Based Combat & Platforming: Your current platform color affects your offensive and defensive abilities. Master the color system to survive.
  • Permadeath Roguelike Progression: One wrong move could mean the end. Push your limits with each run, knowing that failure resets everything.
  • Open-World Void Exploration: Navigate a procedurally generated environment filled with enemies, upgrades, and hidden objects.
  • Character & Platform Upgrades: Improve your abilities, unlock new powers, and strengthen your platforms to survive longer.
  • Charms & Items: Find powerful tools and one-time-use items that give you the edge in life-or-death situations.
  • Retro Visuals: High-contrast 8-bit black and white pixel art style brings a distinct visual identity to the void.
  • Optimized for Browser: Built in Unity and exported to WebGL, playable directly in-browser for instant access.

Release Plans

Contrast is currently in late development, with a focus on adding more content, balancing mechanics, and polishing the core gameplay loop. The game will be launched as a free-to-play title on Itch.io, targeting browser players with a lightweight yet deep roguelike experience.

The initial release is coming soon, featuring the core mechanics, progression system, and early-game content. Post-launch updates are planned to expand the world, introduce new enemies, abilities, and additional platform types for even greater variety and challenge.
