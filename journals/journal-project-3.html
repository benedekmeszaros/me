Floating Base — Endless AFK Base Builder

About the Game

Floating Base is an endless AFK base-building game for mobile devices, designed with a unique 2D vector art style. In this game, players construct and customize a floating fortress using modular gadgets, aiming to survive against increasingly aggressive waves of geometrically-shaped enemies. The gameplay encourages experimentation, allowing players to mix and match gadgets for powerful synergies while upgrading them mid-run to enhance durability and performance. Built for casual yet strategic engagement, Floating Base blends idle progression with real-time tactical decisions.

  • Dynamic Enemies: Face off against a swarm of ever-changing geometric shapes, each behaving differently.
  • Designed for Experimentation: The core gameplay loop encourages creativity and discovery with each attempt.

Release Plans

Originally released in Summer 2021 on the Google Play Store, Floating Base was removed in October 2023 due to inactivity and limited exposure. Despite a promising concept, the lack of marketing and community feedback led to the project being shelved. However, this experience became a valuable lesson on the importance of visibility and long-term planning in game development.

Looking ahead, there are plans to revive and redesign Floating Base with a stronger strategy, improved gameplay loops, and better player engagement in mind. The next version will aim to make a more impactful return—more polished, more strategic, and ready to reach its full potential.
